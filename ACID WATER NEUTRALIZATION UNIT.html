Acid Water Neutralisation Unit

SCOPE: includes design; manufacture; supply and installation supervision of the complete Acidic Water Nutralisation Unit.


The integrated waste acid water neutralization unit includes the following:

  • Treated water evacuation pump(vertical shaft submersible pump of capacity 10m3 /hr). Motor capacity: 3 HP. Necessary bearing housing, shaft extension, bracket for mounting and electrical switchgear will be supplied integrated in the system.
  • PH sensor, controller along with PH value display in the control panel and brackets/ probes etc as necessary.
  • Agitator with SS impeller, 3 hp motor, electrical switch gear, shaft extension, bearing housing, mounting brackets etc.
  • Liquid high/ low level switch on suitable brackets for mounting and adjustment
  • Electrical control panel housing all the switchgears, wired to function in a syncronised manner as per the attached detail. It will also house warning light and hooter.
  • Interfacing cable for motors, limit switches sensors.
  • Pipe line for discharge of treated water to drainage point 10m maximum.
